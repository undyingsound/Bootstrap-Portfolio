About Me

When I was young, I wanted to be the next Dave Grohl. I have an ear for music composition and a drive like no other. This propelled me into school music programs as well as joining or starting bands with anyone that could play an instrument. Unfortunately, I have not yet been indicted into the Music Hall of Fame, but I did become an awesome drummer in the process. However, it is time to find a new careerpath. Rockstar life seems boring anyway...
